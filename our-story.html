Our Story

Our Story

At Lamai Distilleries, we personally ensure that all our products are farm to flask.


With our belief that Thailand has an abundance of ingredients to make great spirits as well as our passion for the country’s heritage, we began operations in 2015 with the sole purpose of creating products that showcased the charm of Thailand.

Lamai Distilleries operates under a local community distillery license in the province of Surin, in the Northeastern part of Thailand. As such, we are a small operation where we are personally responsible for every step of the production process.
