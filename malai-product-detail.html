Malai

-            Malai

Crafted from cane sugar, Malai is perfect for a variety of cocktails. While we are not allowed to call it a rum due to our distilling license, it is a white spirit from sugarcane perfectly complimenting mojitos.

Main Ingredient: Sugarcane

Alcohol Content: 40%

Flavor profile: sweet and aromatic with a slight grassy feel

Classic Mojito

Add two parts lime to one part syrup along with a decent pour of malai along with some mint leaves to make the perfect refresher to any hot day.

Citrus Paradise

Sweet or sour, add freshly squeezed oranges to lime along with Malai for a zesty drink. For fizzle, add some soda.

Mulberry Madness

We grow mulberries so we decided to use them in this refreshing tarty drink. Add two parts mulberries to one part syrup and orange juice with Malai. Top up with soda.

Ingredients

- Lime

- Mint leaves

- Soda

- Syrup
